Suinta Chamber of Commerce Directory

Our Purpose

This website will serve as a resource hub for businesses in the city of Port Moresby. The site will provide information on local events, host networking opportunities, create business directories, and encourage local shopping. It aims to promote local economic growth, support local businesses, and foster a sense of community between the businesses.
